"A team from Federal Student Aid listened to 2,388 calls shorter than 5 minutes between student loan borrowers and Navient, a publicly traded company. It found that in nearly 10 percent of calls, Navient's customer service representatives offered borrowers only the option of "forbearance," which allows them to put off making any payments for a certain period of time."
